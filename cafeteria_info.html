Food Courts

MSRIT has two food courts providing ample choice to students. The two full fledged canteens provide hygienic and sumptuous meals both in North Indian and South Indian styles.

ESB Block Canteen

Timings:

Weekdays: 9 AM to 4:30 PM

Weekends: 9 AM to 2:30 PM

This canteen is present in the Engineering Sciences Block. It features a large variety of foods. It also consists of a juice corner serving various fresh juices.

Multipurpose Block Canteen

Timings:

Weekdays: 9 AM to 4:30 PM

Weekends: 9 AM to 2:30 PM

This canteen is present in the multipurpose block. It features a large variety of foods including various snacks and cakes. Some of the foods provided here are:
